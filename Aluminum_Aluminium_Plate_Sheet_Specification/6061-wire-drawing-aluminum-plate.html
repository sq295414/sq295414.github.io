6061 wire drawing aluminum plate

Source：Original Time：2021-06-25 16:16
Inquiry：

The main alloying elements of 6061 wire drawing aluminum plate are mg and Si, which are widely used. 6061 wire drawing aluminum plate is a manufacturing process of repeatedly scraping out the line of aluminum plate with sandpaper. Its main process is divided into three parts: de esterification, sand mill and water washing. In the drawing process of aluminum plate, the special coating technology after anodizing can make the surface of aluminum plate form a kind of coating layer containing the metal component, which can clearly show every trace of fine and micro wire, so as to make the metal dull with fine and dense hair luster.


6061 wire drawing aluminum plate /p>

Machinability

The t651 state of 6061 is easier to process than that of 5-series alloy. It shows good machinability when machining with single point or axis diamond tools.

Anodizability

6061 alloy has excellent surface treatment and anodizing properties, especially for hard oxidation.

Corrosion resistance

6061 alloy is an aluminum alloy with high corrosion resistance. The results show that the alloy has good wave corrosion in air and water, almost no stress corrosion fracture and flake spalling. The corrosion resistance of 6061 alloy will not decrease when exposed to high temperature.

Weldability

6061 alloy has good weldability and can be welded by various welding methods. In the heat affected area, the tensile strength will be reduced to 165-200mpa by welding. Its properties can be partially recovered by RE heat treatment, and the corrosion resistance of the weld is also good.
