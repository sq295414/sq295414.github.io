Construction Aluminium plate

    Aluminum occupies advantage position in modern construction applications, such as its excellent formability and aesthetics. Moreover, aluminum possesses another most important advantage: recyclable. Nowadays, 95% aluminum products, which are used in constructions, are recyclable to reproduce the products with the same quality. With the rise of “green building” in every corner of the world, aluminum products are widely used in building and construction industries. By using aluminum, the following advantages can be obtained: 1) enhance the efficiency of energy; 2) reduce the generation of CO2.



    HWALU aluminum is capable of providing construction industries with Aluminium plate with high surface reflectivity, which is able to increase light management efficiency and decrease energy consumption. For instance, keep the building cool in hot season. We manufacture construction-used Aluminium plate products which are water-proof, corrosion resistant and anti-ultraviolet, ensuring their excellent performance during long-term service.

Classic alloy types :  1050, 1100, 5005, 5052, 5182, 6061, 6063, 6N01.

Product character :  water-proof, corrosion resistance, anti-ultraviolet.

Packing
Aluminium plate : Aluminum Plates are placed in wooden boxes on wooden skids with runners along the length of the sheet. They are wrapped in HDPE with hardboard on top and bottom of the stack. Hoop iron strapping runs along the length of the sheets. Silica gel packets are put in the boxes for moisture protection.
