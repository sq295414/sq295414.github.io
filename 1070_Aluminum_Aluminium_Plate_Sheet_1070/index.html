Alloy 1070 Aluminum Plate Sheet

    1070 aluminum plate sheet because its purity is higher up to 99.7%, so it is suitable for electrical and chemical applications utilizing aluminum base metal with little or no alloying elements. It also has resistant to chemical corrosion and has good crack resistance. Such aluminum plate sheet is widely used in buss bars、electrical boxes、heat ex-changers、metallizing、electrical, chemical, construction and food industry, and low strength corrosion resistant vessels and tanks.


 
Alloy: 1070
Temper: O, H111, H112, H12,H14, H16, H18, H22, H24, H26
Thickness: 0.2mm--200mm
With: 500mm--2000mm
Length: 1000mm--12000mm
Surface: Mill finish, Bright finish, paper interleaved, one side film, both sides film.
